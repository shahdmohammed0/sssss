LÉONIE branch in Egypt

Most productive

LEONI's Egypt site, launched in 1998 as a manufacturer of cable harnesses and commercial wiring systems, now employs around 6200 workers across 15 factories in Cairo's free zone district, Asyut, and Badr city.

LÉONIE branch in germany

The company's main branch

Leoni AG, based in Nuremberg, Germany, is a cable and harnessing manufacturing firm with branches throughout the world. It is listed on the Frankfurt Stock Exchange and is a member of the mid-cap MDAX index, and is claimed as global leader of cable systems.
